Child Minding Course:

Nurturing Young Minds

Course Overview:

At Empowering The Nation, we offer a comprehensive Child Minding Course designed for caregivers and parents who want to provide the best care for children from birth to toddler age. Our expert instructors will guide you through the developmental needs of young children, ensuring you feel confident and prepared to nurture their growth.

What You’ll Learn:

1. Birth to Six-Month-Old Baby Needs:

  • Understand the physical, emotional, and developmental needs of infants.
  • Learn about feeding routines, sleep patterns, and hygiene practices essential for newborns.
  • Gain insights into bonding techniques and recognizing developmental milestones during the first six months.

2. Seven-Month to One-Year-Old Needs:

  • Discover the changing needs of babies as they approach their first year.
  • Learn about introducing solid foods, creating a safe exploration environment, and fostering early communication.
  • Understand the importance of routines and how they contribute to a baby’s security and development.

3. Toddler Needs:

  • Explore the unique developmental milestones of toddlers, including motor skills, language development, and social interactions.
  • Learn effective strategies for managing tantrums, encouraging independence, and promoting healthy habits.
  • Understand the importance of play in a toddler’s learning and emotional development.

4. Educational Toys:

  • Discover the role of educational toys in child development and how they can stimulate learning through play.
  • Learn how to choose age-appropriate toys that promote cognitive, motor, and social skills.
  • Gain practical tips on incorporating educational play into daily routines to foster creativity and learning.

Course Structure:

  • Duration: 6 Weeks
  • Format: In person training
  • Class Size: Class size will vary due to your skill level. Beginners can expect to be in a class of 30.
  • Materials Provided: All materials are supplied at our centre but you are welcome to bring your own

Who can join?

This course is ideal for parents, caregivers, and anyone interested in enhancing their understanding of child development and effective caregiving.
