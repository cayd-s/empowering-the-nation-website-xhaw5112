Garden Maintenance Course:

Cultivate Your Green Thumb

Course Overview:

Join us at Empowering The Nation for our Garden Maintenance Course, where you’ll learn essential skills to keep your garden thriving. This course is perfect for both novice gardeners and experienced green thumbs looking to enhance their gardening knowledge and practices.

What You’ll Learn:

1. Water Restrictions and the Watering Requirements of Indigenous and Exotic Plants:

  • Understand local water restrictions and how they impact garden maintenance.
  • Learn the specific watering needs of indigenous versus exotic plants to promote healthy growth.
  • Discover efficient watering techniques, including drip irrigation and rainwater harvesting, to conserve water while maintaining a vibrant garden.

2. Pruning and Propagation of Plants:

  • Master the art of pruning to enhance plant health, shape, and flowering.
  • Learn the best times and methods for pruning various plant types, from shrubs to trees.
  • Explore propagation techniques, including cuttings, division, and layering, to expand your garden and share plants with friends.

3. Planting Techniques for Different Plant Types:

  • Discover the fundamentals of planting, including soil preparation and site selection.
  • Learn the appropriate techniques for planting seeds, bulbs, and transplants to ensure strong root development.
  • Understand spacing and companion planting to maximize garden productivity and health.

Course Structure:

  • Duration: 6 Weeks
  • Format: In person training
  • Class Size: Class size will vary due to your skill level. Beginners can expect to be in a class of 30.
  • Materials Provided: All materials are supplied at our centre but you are welcome to bring your own

Who can join?

This course is ideal for anyone interested in improving their gardening skills, from homeowners to aspiring landscape professionals.
