Cooking Course:

Nourish Your Body and Mind

Course Overview:

    At Empowering The Nation, we invite you to join our comprehensive Cooking Course, where you’ll learn the essentials of healthy cooking and meal planning. Whether you’re a beginner or looking to enhance your culinary skills, our experienced instructors will guide you through the journey of preparing nutritious and delicious meals.

What You’ll Learn:

1. Nutritional Requirements for a Healthy Body:

  • Understand the fundamentals of nutrition and the importance of a balanced diet.
  • Learn about essential vitamins, minerals, and macronutrients necessary for optimal health.
  • Discover how to create meals that meet dietary needs and promote overall well-being

2. Types of Protein, Carbohydrates, and Vegetables:

  • Explore different sources of protein, including meats, legumes, and plant-based options.
  • Understand the role of carbohydrates and how to choose healthy options, such as whole grains and fruits.
  • Learn about a variety of vegetables, their nutritional benefits, and how to incorporate them into your meals.

3. Planning Meals:

  • Master the art of meal planning to save time and reduce food waste.
  • Learn how to create balanced meal plans that cater to dietary preferences and restrictions.
  • Discover tips for grocery shopping, budgeting, and organizing your kitchen for efficiency.

4. Preparation and Cooking of Meals:

  • Gain hands-on experience in food preparation techniques, including chopping, marinating, and seasoning.
  • Learn various cooking methods such as steaming, roasting, sautéing, and grilling.
  • Explore recipes that highlight seasonal ingredients and encourage creativity in the kitchen.

Course Structure:

  • Duration: 6 Weeks
  • Format: In person training
  • Class Size: Class size will vary due to your skill level. Beginners can expect to be in a class of 30.
  • Materials Provided: All materials are supplied at our centre but you are welcome to bring your own

Who can join?

This course is perfect for anyone interested in cooking, from beginners to those looking to enhance their culinary skills and knowledge about nutrition
