First Aid Course

Be Prepared to Save Lives

Course Overview:

At Empowering The Nation, we offer a comprehensive First Aid Course designed to equip you with essential skills and knowledge to respond effectively in emergencies. Whether at home, work, or in the community, being prepared can make all the difference. Join us and learn how to save lives!

What You’ll Learn:

1. Wounds and Bleeding:

  • Understand different types of wounds and how to assess their severity.
  • Learn techniques for controlling bleeding, including direct pressure and the use of dressings.
  • Gain knowledge of infection prevention and proper wound care to promote healing.

2. Burns and Fractures:

  • Identify various types of burns (thermal, chemical, electrical) and appropriate first aid responses.
  • Learn how to assess and immobilize fractures to prevent further injury.
  • Understand when to seek medical help for burns and fractures.

3. Emergency Scene Management:

  • Learn how to assess an emergency scene for safety before providing aid.
  • Understand the importance of calling for help and communicating effectively with emergency services.
  • Gain skills in prioritizing care for multiple victims and managing stress in high-pressure situations..

4. Cardio-Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR):

  • Master the steps of CPR for adults, children, and infants, including chest compressions and rescue breaths.
  • Learn how to use an Automated External Defibrillator (AED) effectively.
  • Practice hands-on techniques to build confidence in performing CPR

5. Respiratory Distress:

  • Recognize the signs and symptoms of respiratory distress and common causes.
  • Learn first aid interventions for conditions such as choking and asthma attacks.
  • Understand when to seek emergency medical assistance for respiratory issues.

Course Structure:

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Format: In person training
  • Class Size: Class size will vary due to your skill level. Beginners can expect to be in a class of 30.
  • Materials Provided: All materials are supplied at our centre but you are welcome to bring your own

Who can join?

This course is suitable for anyone interested in learning first aid skills, including parents, teachers, caregivers, and workplace safety officers.
