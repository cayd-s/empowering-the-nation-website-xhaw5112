Landscaping Course:

Transform Your Outdoor Space

Course Overview:

Join us at Empowering The Nation for an immersive Landscaping Course designed to help you create beautiful, functional outdoor spaces. Whether you're a gardening enthusiast or looking to start a new hobby, our expert instructors will guide you through essential landscaping principles and techniques.

What You’ll Learn:

1. Indigenous and Exotic Plants and Trees:

  • Discover the benefits of incorporating indigenous plants into your landscape, including their low maintenance and ecological advantages.
  • Learn about exotic plants and how to choose varieties that thrive in your local environment
  • Gain insights into the appropriate care and placement of various plant species to enhance biodiversity.

2. Fixed Structures:

  • Explore the role of fixed structures in landscape design, including fountains, statues, benches, tables, and built-in braais.
  • Learn how to select and place these features to create focal points and enhance the functionality of your outdoor space.
  • Understand maintenance considerations for different materials and structures.

3. Balancing of Plants and Trees in a Garden:

  • Master the art of balancing various plant sizes, shapes, and colors to create a harmonious garden.
  • Learn techniques for layering plants and trees to achieve depth and visual interest.
  • Explore companion planting and how to create a thriving ecosystem in your garden.
  • 4. Aesthetics of Plant Shapes and Colors:
    • Understand the impact of plant shapes and colors on the overall aesthetics of your landscape
    • Learn how to choose color palettes and shapes that complement your garden style and personal taste.
    • Experiment with seasonal planting to ensure year-round beauty.

    • 5. Garden Layout:

    • Discover effective garden layout principles, including symmetry, pathways, and zoning..
    • Learn how to create functional spaces for relaxation, entertainment, and play.
    • Gain skills in using design software or sketching techniques to plan your ideal landscape.

    Course Structure:

    • Duration: 6 Months
    • Format: In person training
    • Class Size: Class size will vary due to your skill level. Beginners can expect to be in a class of 30.
    • Materials Provided: All materials are supplied at our centre but you are welcome to bring your own

    Who can join?

    This course is perfect for gardening enthusiasts, homeowners, and anyone looking to improve their landscaping knowledge and skills.
