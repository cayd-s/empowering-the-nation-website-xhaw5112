Life Skills Course

Empower Your Future

Course Overview:

At Empowering The Nation, we believe that life skills are essential for personal and professional success. Our comprehensive Life Skills Course covers crucial topics to help you navigate everyday challenges with confidence. Join us to build a strong foundation for a successful future!

What You’ll Learn:

1. Opening a Bank Account

  • Understand the different types of bank accounts and their purposes.
  • Learn how to choose the right bank and account for your needs.
  • Step-by-step guidance on the account opening process, including necessary documents and requirements.
  • Tips on managing your account, including online banking and budgeting basics.

. Basic Labour Law (Know Your Rights):

  • Gain an understanding of your rights as an employee, including wages, working hours, and safe working conditions.
  • Learn how to identify and report workplace discrimination or harassment
  • Familiarize yourself with important labour laws and protections in your area.
  • Understand the resources available for legal support and guidance.

3. Basic Reading and Writing Literacy:

  • Improve your reading comprehension skills to better understand everyday documents, including job applications and contracts.
  • Enhance your writing skills, focusing on clear and effective communication for personal and professional contexts.
  • Engage in practical exercises to boost your confidence in both reading and writing.

4. Basic Numeric Literacy:

  • Build foundational math skills necessary for managing personal finances, such as budgeting, calculating expenses, and understanding interest rates
  • Learn how to interpret numerical data and graphs commonly encountered in everyday life
  • Practice real-life scenarios to enhance your comfort with numbers and basic calculations.

Course Structure:

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Format: In person training
  • Class Size: Class size will vary due to your skill level. Beginners can expect to be in a class of 30.
  • Materials Provided: All materials are supplied at our centre but you are welcome to bring your own

Who can join?

This course is designed for anyone looking to enhance their life skills, whether you're a student, recent graduate, or simply seeking personal development.
